HMUN 2015 TIMELINE

Registration for AMIMUN 2015 opened on October 12th, 2014! Click here to access our online database for registration.

The First Round Registration Deadline for AMIMUN 2015 was on October 26th. AMIMUN has the second Round Regsitration from November 16th till December 6th, 2014. Application received between November 16th and December 6th, will be reviewed and acceptance decision released on a rolling basis. Country assignments for all application received between November 16th and December 6th will be considered together and released by December 13th.

Second Round Registration for The Game of Thrones Committee has been extended till December 15th, 2014.

Delegates applying for Ad-Hoc Crisis Committee will only receive the committee topic and background guide a week before the conference

The third and final round for registration will start from 16th December. The final deadline for registering for the conference is December 31st, 2014

CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM: Are you an enthusiastic MUNer? Are you interested in training and recruiting delegates? Do you have fun organising on and off campus activities? If you are, then the AMIMUN campus ambassador program wants you. Amity University is giving you an opportunity to act as liasons between them and your college and to recruite delegates to take part in AMIMUN'15. AMIMUN Campus Ambassadors can be undergraduate/graduate/post-graduate students studying in that university. This role is a volunteer position wherein exciting prizes and appreciation certificates will be awarded to the most forthcoming ambassadors. So help us spread the word and drive the AMIMUN initiative in your college.
